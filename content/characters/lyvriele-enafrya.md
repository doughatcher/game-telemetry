Lyvriele Enafrya moves through the world with the particular bearing of someone who has learned that elegance and survival are not mutually exclusive. An Eladrin fighter trained in the Samurai tradition — a discipline about presence as much as technique, about the unsheathed quality of attention a warrior brings to everything, not just combat.

She watched the arrival at Shard C with the particular stillness of someone committing a place to memory before it changes. The Sea of Governance is named for a kind of order — structured, institutional, the sort of power that does not announce itself with thunder but with procedure. For someone of Eladrin sensibility, that kind of power is neither foreign nor comfortable. It is simply another thing to read correctly.

The party rested and equipped itself in the margins between arrival and action. Lyvriele attended to those preparations with the same focused quiet she brings to everything else. Among the group, she is the one you notice not because she demands it, but because she cannot help it. Whatever the Sea of Governance intends to ask of them, she will have already considered the question.
