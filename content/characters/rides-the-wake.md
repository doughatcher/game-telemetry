Rides the Wake is a barbarian who carries herself like someone accustomed to moving through rough water — patient in stillness, overwhelming in motion. She is not reckless in the way people imagine barbarians to be; there is a current of deliberate purpose beneath the fury, a sense that the rage, when it comes, has been earned. Those who travel with her describe a quiet confidence that borders on the serene, right up until it doesn't.

This session, the party reached the Shard C — the Sea of Governance — a destination that has apparently taken no small effort to arrive at. Rides the Wake was present for that arrival, and whatever it means to stand at the edge of a sea that governs, she seemed neither awed nor dismissive. Simply ready. The name of the place suits someone like her: governance, after all, is just another word for the force that keeps things from flying apart.
