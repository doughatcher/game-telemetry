Vargr Stormcaller gives the impression of someone who has seen enough of the world to be skeptical of it, but not so much as to have stopped caring. A Goliath bard of the College of Spirits — a strange combination that somehow makes complete sense once you encounter it. The spirits speak to those who are large enough to hold them, and Vargr has always been that.

Near the stern at Shard C, speaking in low tones with Edwin, there was the quality of someone processing a place by talking around it. The Sea of Governance is a place where power takes on formal shape, and it remains to be seen how Vargr will reckon with that. The name "Stormcaller" suggests a history with weather — perhaps literal, perhaps metaphorical. Either way: a force that has learned to perform its nature.
